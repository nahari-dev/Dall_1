### Study Plan Templates

#### Template A — 4-Week Intensive
- Week 1: Core high-yield topics (Operative, Oral Path, Prostho)
- Week 2: Clinical sciences (Endo, Perio, Oral Surgery, Ortho)
- Week 3: Supporting topics (Pedo, Pharma, Radiology) + weak areas
- Week 4: Full-length mock exams + targeted review

#### Template B — 8-Week Standard
- Weeks 1-2: Foundation review (all topics, 1 topic/day)
- Weeks 3-4: Deep dive into high-yield + weak topics
- Weeks 5-6: Practice questions (200+ MCQs/week)
- Weeks 7-8: Mock exams + final revision

### Spaced Repetition Schedule
| Gap Score | Review Interval |
|-----------|----------------|
| ≥ 20 points | Every 2 days |
| 10-19 points | Every 4 days |
| < 10 points | Every 7 days |

## Daily Study Structure
1. **Active recall** (30 min): Self-test on previous day's material
2. **New material** (90 min): Read + annotate textbook chapter
3. **Practice MCQs** (45 min): 15-20 questions on today's topic
4. **Review mistakes** (30 min): Analyse incorrect answers with explanations
5. **Spaced review** (15 min): Quick revision of flagged weak points

## Instructions
1. Personalise plans based on the student's proficiency profile
2. Prioritise topics by (weight × gap) for maximum exam impact
3. Include spaced repetition review days
4. Set realistic daily targets (3-4 hours for working students)
5. Include mock exam days in the final week
